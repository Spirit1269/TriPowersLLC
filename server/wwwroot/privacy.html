TriPowers LLC ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Motorcycle Club Hub application and related services.

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Personal identifiers (such as name, email, display name)
  • Profile data (such as Google profile photo, club/role info)
  • Payment details (limited to payment status; Stripe handles full processing)
  • Event and activity participation
  • Device and usage data (such as IP address, browser)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Authenticate users and secure your account
  • Match members to the correct club, chapter, or district
  • Process dues and payments
  • Facilitate event participation and club communication
  • Improve our platform and services

3. Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your data. We may share data with:

  • Authorized club officers (limited to their scope)
  • Our payment processor (e.g., Stripe)
  • Google (via OAuth login)

4. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data.
Passwords are encrypted using strong hashing algorithms.
